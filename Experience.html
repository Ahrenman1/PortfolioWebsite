Work Experience

As the second busiest Co-operative store in Scotland, I was able to gain valuable experience working in a fast paced environment, whilst also being integrated with strong team of 20 who I formed very close friendships with. It was always improtant to me to come in every day with a positive attitude, and maintain this even when things did not go according to plan.

On first glance, a job in retail might seem very different to technical roles within an engineering company, but the vast variety of transferable skills I developed while at the Co-op is something which has benefitted me immensely throughout University and social life, and I am certain these skills will be easily applicable to engineering roles. Over the summers, I served thousands of customers and worked with incredible people. My salesmanship and ability to adapt to different personalities can be applied anywhere, along with my commitment and organisation to tasks to ensure they were completed on time and to a high standard.

Not only did my verbal communication improve substantially, my ability to listen was also heightened. I became more confident, efficient and flexible around the shops variety of working hours, being on hand to help out when others were overwhelmed with the demands of the day.
