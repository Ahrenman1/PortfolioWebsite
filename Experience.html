As the second busiest Co-operative store in Scotland, I was able to gain valuable experience working in a fast paced environment, whilst also being integrated with strong team of 20 who I formed very close friendships with. It was always important to me to come in every day with a positive attitude, and maintain this even when things did not go according to plan.

There is a reason I chose to spent three summers at the Co-op. On first glance, a job in retail might seem very different to technical roles within an engineering company, but the vast variety of transferable skills I developed while at the Co-op is something which has benefitted me immensely throughout University and social life, and I am certain these skills will be easily applicable to engineering roles. Over these summers, I served thousands of customers and worked with incredible people. My salesmanship and ability to adapt to different personalities can be applied anywhere, along with my commitment and organisation to tasks to ensure they were completed on time and to a high standard.

Not only did my verbal communication improve substantially, my ability to listen was also heightened. I became more confident, efficient and flexible around the shops variety of working hours, being on hand to help out when others were overwhelmed with the demands of the day.

Extra

In 2014, I was one of the few students across Scotland selected to attend the Scottish Space School. It had a large effect on my confidence, and helped solidify my aspirations to work in engineering. The week consisted of group activities, getting presentations from astronauts, engineers and scientists from NASA, and gaining invaluable experience of being a student and learning about the University of Strathclyde.

One of the most important aspects was getting to meet likeminded people from across the Scotland and the UK. Learning about the impact of the space industry, and the emerging technical capability of the Scottish Space sector, was very exciting. The experience still effects me positively to this day, and I gained many friendships that have lasted ever since then.
